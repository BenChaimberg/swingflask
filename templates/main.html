Our Mission

Swing Paints Ltd. has been manufacturing paint specialty and wood refinishing products for the last 50 years. We are a family-owned and operated business. As a result, we take a very hands-on approach to every aspect of our business - from the raw materials to the labels on the cans themselves. Our goal is to produce the highest quality products at the most competitieve prices.
