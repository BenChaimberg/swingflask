Our Mission

Swing Paints Limited is a family-owned and operated business located in Montreal, Canada. We have been manufacturing paints, varnishes, and wood refinishing products for the last 50 years. We take a very hands-on approach to every aspect of our business - from the raw materials to the look of each label. Our goal is to produce the highest quality products at the most competitive prices.
